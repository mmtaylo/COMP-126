Here are the rules:

  1. you must use flexbox for pretty much all the positioning and spacing
  2. you may NOT use floats or positioning properties (relative, absolute, fixed)
  3. you may NOT add width, max-width, height, or max-height to anything; you may change the value of the max-width property already in the code, but that's it
  4. you may NOT use the text-align property
  5. you may NOT add margins
  6. you MAY add padding
  7. you MAY add new classes for styling if you want
  8. you may NOT add anything other than class names to the HTML
  9. everything must be centered as pictured, including the text within the nav items
  10. you will definitely be nesting flex containers and items
flexbox isn't the only way to create this navbar, but it's the way we're doing it today
